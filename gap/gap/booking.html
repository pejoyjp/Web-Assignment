GAP Summer Family Fun Days


GAP regularly hosts a Family Fun day during the Summer months. Families are welcome to attend and
enjoy a day of food, music, games and activities with all the thoughtful and considered support you would
expect from GAP.


Relax while your children enjoy a day of fun and play and meet other families.


We have been very lucky to host some amazing offerings like BBQ trucks, ice cream vans, a visit from a
farm, bouncy castles, magicians, musicians and even a real life mermaid!


GAP would like to thank everyone at the Walled Garden in Kilcornan, Clarinbridge who volunteered the
use of their amazing space in 2019.
